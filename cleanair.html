Cutting-edge solutions to reduce emissions from conventional vehicles.

Diesel Particulate Filters (DPF)

Advanced filtration systems that remove 95% of particulate matter from diesel engines.

  • Efficiency:95%
  • Cost:Rs: 50,000 - 80,000
  • Installation:2-3 Hours
  • Applications:Trucks, Buses, Generators

Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)

Reduces NOx emissions up to 90% using urea injection technology.

  • Efficiency:90%
  • Cost:Rs: 75,000 - 120,000
  • Installation:4-6 Hours
  • Applications:Heavy Vehicles, Industrial Equipment

Catalytic Converters

High efficiency converters for petrol engines reducing CO and HC emissions.

  • Efficiency:85%
  • Cost:Rs: 15,000 - 35,000
  • Installation:1-2 Hours
  • Applications:Cars, Motorcycles, CNG vehicles
